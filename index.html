Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

World History Slots (“the App”) is designed to help students prepare for the AP World History exam. This privacy policy explains how the App handles information.

Information We Collect

World History Slots does not collect any personal information from its users. All study progress, streak data, and practice results are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to any server.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information to serve relevant ads, including device identifiers and usage data. This data collection is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Through Google AdMob, the following data types may be collected for advertising purposes:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • Coarse location (derived from IP address)
  • Usage data (such as app interactions)
  • Diagnostic data

You will be asked for permission to track your activity across apps and websites when you first open the app. You can change this at any time in iPhone Settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for students preparing for the AP World History exam. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. All data is stored locally on your device.

Third Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
