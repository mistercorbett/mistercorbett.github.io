Privacy Policy

AP World History Slots
Last updated: April 2026

World History Slots (“the App”) is designed to help students prepare for the AP World History exam. This privacy policy explains how the App handles information.

Information We Collect

World History Slots does not collect any personal information from its users. All study progress, streak data, and practice results are stored locally on your device only.

AI Grading Feature

The App’s SAQ MODE feature uses the Anthropic API to grade student responses. When a student submits a Short Answer Question for grading, their written response is sent to Anthropic’s servers for processing. This data is used solely to provide grading feedback and is governed by Anthropic’s Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy. No personally identifiable information is included in grading requests — only the student’s written response and the question context.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for students preparing for the AP World History exam. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. Study data is stored locally on your device and grading responses are processed anonymously through the Anthropic API.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.

Contact

Privacy Policy

Tomato Journal
Last updated: May 2026

Tomato Journal (“the App”) is a personal garden tracking app for tomato growers. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

Tomato Journal stores your garden data — plants, harvests, journal entries, and photos — in your personal iCloud account via Apple’s CloudKit. This data is yours, stored under your Apple ID, and is never accessed by us.

Subscription

Tomato Journal uses Apple’s StoreKit for subscription management. We do not collect or store any payment information. All billing is handled entirely by Apple.

What We Don’t Do

We do not collect personal information. We do not serve ads. We do not share your data with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions please contact us at: mistercorbett@gmail.com

“The tomato app — for tomato people ”

For hobbyist tomato gardeners who want to build a personal record they can reference and improve on each season.

// What It Does

Your tomato season, remembered.

🍅
Plant Tracking

Log every variety by name, type, characteristics, date planted, bed, and region. Arrange plants in walk order. Quick-add from varieties you've grown before.

⚖️
Harvest Log

Record harvests per plant as they happen. Season totals and lifetime yields give you the full picture of what your garden produces.

📓
Journal Entries

Attach notes and photos to any plant. Dictate with speech-to-text. Pest issues, taste impressions, anything worth remembering for next year.

📚
Variety Encyclopedia

A library of every variety you've ever grown — lifetime stats, season history, ratings, and photos, grouped by color, size, or growth habit.

📅
Season Comparison

Year-over-year summaries with highlights, variety ratings, and honest notes on what you'd grow again — and what you wouldn't. Export to CSV anytime.

☁️
iCloud Sync

All your data — plants, harvests, journal entries, and photos — syncs automatically across your iPhone and iPad via iCloud.

// Four Tabs

Four tabs. One complete season record.

Garden
Yields
Seasons
Varieties
Garden

Your living list of every plant in the ground this season. Add a variety, log when it went in, note the bed, and update its status as the season progresses.

  • Add varieties by name, type, characteristics, and region
  • Track planting date, bed location, and plant status
  • Log harvests and journal entries (with photos and speech-to-text) per plant
  • Track seed saving per plant
  • Arrange plants in walk order; quick-add from previously grown varieties
Yields

A clean harvest summary filtered by season. See what you pulled out of the garden, plant by plant and in total — with lifetime numbers in the All Seasons view.

  • Season-filtered harvest summary
  • Grouped harvest log by plant and date
  • Lifetime totals across all seasons
  • Compare yields across varieties at a glance
Seasons

The long view. Year-over-year comparisons with highlights, ratings, and the honest record of what worked and what didn't.

  • Year-over-year season comparison
  • Season highlights — best tasting, would/wouldn't grow again
  • Variety ratings per season
  • CSV export of your full harvest record
Varieties

A read-only encyclopedia of every variety you've ever grown, built automatically from your data across all seasons. The longer you use the app, the richer this gets.

  • Lifetime stats, season history, and ratings per variety
  • Journal entries and photos aggregated across all seasons
  • Group by color, size, growth habit, or plant type
  • Grows more useful every season you track
